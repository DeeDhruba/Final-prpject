Our Mission

At Eastmont Community Center, we believe in nurturing community strength by addressing food insecurity and providing comprehensive social services. Our mission is to empower residents through education, wellness, and support programs, fostering self-sufficiency and enhancing quality of life in East LA.

Families Helped

Each year, 5,000 families depend on our organization to transform their aspirations into tangible achievements. We stand at the forefront of providing critical support, whether it's through educational opportunities, housing assistance, or financial guidance.

Other Ways We Help

Our Citizenship Classes boast a remarkable 92% pass rate, demonstrating our commitment to empowering individuals on their journey to becoming new citizens. This success is a testament to our tailored teaching methods, comprehensive curriculum, and the dedicated support of our experienced instructors.

We Care About Your Health, Not Just the Food You Eat

For those facing financial barriers, our monthly health screenings at the site provide a lifeline. By offering free access to physical exams, vaccinations, and check-ups, we help ensure that everyone, regardless of economic status, has the opportunity to maintain their health.
